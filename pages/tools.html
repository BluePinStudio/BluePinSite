ProductiviTree

A task tracker that resembles an RPG skill tree. This helps visualize dependencies and available task at a glance without using complex lists.

Steam Tag Hunter

A search tool for uncovering hidden patterns across Steam tags. Compare genres, find niche games, and track the profitability of tag combinations used by successful games.

Steam Dev/Pub Leaderboards

A leaderboard ranking developers and publishers by estimated revenue, game released count, and median game performance. A quick way to see the who's who of Steam developers and publishers.

Steam Gumball Machine

Put a coin in the gumball machine and get a gumball. Discover random Steam games according to your own filters. Imagine the steam recommendation algorithm without the algorithm.
