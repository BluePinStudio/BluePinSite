Tools

Tag Hunter

Very powerful tool for finding lookalike games. Finds games that Valve themselves would never surface to you. It can auto-populate tags from existing games or you can manually input tags to suit your needs.

ProductiviTree

A task tracker that resembles an RPG skill tree. Visualize dependencies and available tasks at a glance without digging through lists.

Steam Boards

A powerful tool for researching Steam developers and publishers.

Tag Boards

Look up Steam tags for insight regarding revenue by tag. Comes with 10 different sorting options and banned-tag capabilities!
