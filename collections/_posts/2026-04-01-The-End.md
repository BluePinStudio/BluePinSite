## <span style="color:#007bff">Welcome Back!</span>

This is the final blog post... It has been an absolute pleasure to try gamedev and get all your support for this long. Me and Will, the other half of Blue Pin Studio published our first game on itch in March 2020. 

I figured there's no better time to contemplate the past than every year in March. This time I decided enough is enough. 

In the beginning many people were supportive but as I sunk more and more time without financial success I started getting concerned comments. I had falling outs with family, friends, and even lost a long term relationship partially because of who I became chasing game dev success.

I can't even tell you how many people told me to stop, to slow down, to focus on getting a real career, etc.

I know their heart was in a good place. It's time i listened...

But before we go, I would love to reflect on all the positive comments people have left on all my games. I also want to write a special thank you to everyone who helped me on each of the games. I made an effort to work with and meet as many great people as I could. 

I'm honored that all of these great people carved out at least a small part of their time, effort, emtions and energy for me...
